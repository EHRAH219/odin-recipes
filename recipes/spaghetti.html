Spaghetti

Description

A hamburger and marina based spaghetti dish.

Ingredients

  • Spagetti noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Marina sauce
  • Desired Vegetables
  • Desired Seasonings

Steps

  1. Grab sauce pan.
  2. Add marina sauce, meat, and desired seasonings or vegetables.
  3. Place pan on stove burner and turn to medium heat.
  4. Stir ingredients together until warm.
  5. Turn burner to low heat and let simmer, continue to stir.
  6. Grab pot and fill halfway up with water.
  7. Place pot on burner and turn to high heat.
  8. When boiling add noodles to water.
  9. Turn down heat to medium and stir noodles.
  10. Heat until desired texture is met.
  11. Strain noodles.
  12. Combine both sauce and noodles into bowl.
  13. Serve.
