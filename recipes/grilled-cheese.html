Grilled Cheese

Description

A grilled cheese sandwhich.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese Block
  • Butter
  • Optional Lunchmeat

Steps

  1. Grab flat skillet.
  2. Place skillet on burner and set burner to medium low.
  3. Layer one side of bread with butter, and place it onto skillet butter side down.
  4. Add cheese and lunch meat to bread.
  5. Layer another piece of bread with butter, place this on top of the sandwhich butter side up.
  6. Wait 30 seconds to a minute flip sandwhich.
  7. Wait another 30 seconds to a minute.
  8. If toastier bread is desired flip over once more repeating process with other side.
  9. If sandwhich is as desired remove from skillet.
  10. Serve
