Top Ramen

Description

A simple top ramen recipe.

Ingredients

  • Top Ramen
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Grab pot and fill halfway with water.
  2. Place pot on stove and set burner to high.
  3. Cut up onion.
  4. When water begins to boil turn burner to medium.
  5. Add ramen noodles from package, and throw away season packet.
  6. Strain any unwanted water from noodles when they meet desired texture.
  7. Add seasonings and onion.
  8. Serve.
